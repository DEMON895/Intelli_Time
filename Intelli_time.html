How IntelliTime Works

Our proprietary algorithm follows a multi-stage optimization process.

1. Data Collection

Our system gathers all relevant inputs - courses, rooms, instructors, student enrollments, and any special constraints or preferences.

2. Initial Constraint Satisfaction

The algorithm first eliminates impossible combinations to create a feasible solution space.

3. Multi-objective Optimization

Using genetic algorithms and machine learning, we optimize for multiple factors simultaneously while respecting all constraints.

4. Human-in-the-Loop Review

The system presents options to administrators who can provide feedback that improves future optimizations.

5. Continuous Adaptation

As schedules are used and modified, the system learns and adapts to changing circumstances and preferences.
